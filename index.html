Who are we?


Founded in 2016 in Dublin, Benfont Limited started out creating mobile games for iOS. This independent design studio has extended its portfolio over the last year into mobile app development.

What have we achieved?


Benfont Limited launched several games throughout 2016/17, including the popular game Arctic Smash, which was picked by Apple as game of the day during January and February 2018 - reaching the number one spot in the adventure games in over 40 countries!

What is our goal?


Today, the studio focusses on the creation, development and distribution of high quality and engaging mobile games and applications with a view to collaborate with companies and studios to develop bespoke mobile applications for a variety of industries.
